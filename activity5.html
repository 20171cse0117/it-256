Pichai Sundararajan (born June 10, 1972[3]), also known as Sundar Pichai (/'s?nd??r p?'t?a?/), is an Indian American business executive,[4] the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC.[5][6][7] Pichai began his career as a materials engineer and joined Google as a management executive in 2004. He rose to become the company's Product Chief, then Google's CEO in 2015, as part of the restructuring process that made Alphabet Inc. into Google's parent company.[8][9] In December 2019, he additionally became CEO of Alphabet Inc. ichai was born in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India.[12][13] His mother Lakshmi was a stenographer and his father, Regunatha Pichai was an electrical engineer at GEC, the British conglomerate.
